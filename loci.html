Avatar Genius Loci - Ride Queue to Pandora's Flight of Passage

Pa ✦ Mer

Syracuse University ✦ Sophomore Fall 2025

Genius Loci means the spirit of a place and in this project we were asked to think of a place that gives us an intense positive emotion, and that place is Disney’s Pandora in Animal Kingdom. For this particular assignment, we were asked to make a 3D collage journey that abstracted the place we chose into elements instead of direct representations. Materials, colors, patterns, form, line, space, what makes the essence of the space that made us feel so positively? For Flight of Passage, the ride queue’s details are what inspired me to create this piece. The four main areas are the cave, the forest, the lab, and the basement just before the ride. I used different materials from our scrap bins to create a journey that represented the journey through the ride queue as you walk to the ride itself. Indesign, Sketching, Concept Design, Material Board

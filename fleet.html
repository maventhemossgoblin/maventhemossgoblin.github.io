Fleet Farming Internship

Merchandise Design

Internship ✦ Orlando, FL 2021

I created these merchandise designs for the company Fleet Farming. They are a daughter company from Ideas for Us. Both are based in the Orlando/Audubon area. Fleet Farming is trying to change the cycle of food through education and initiation. They sell their farm grown produce at the farmers market and have options to turn your lawn into a food haven! Once a month, they host a hive meeting with the community and they ride through the streets to each farm they have created to gather excess food to sell or repurpose. They are currently targeting lawns and trying to get people to move from grass lawns to food lawns. I was tasked with creating merchandise for their shop to help raise funds to continue the cause. Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Placeit

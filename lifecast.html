Life Cast Magazine & Convention

Editorial/Publication Design ✦ Environmental/Experiential Design ✦ Brand Identity

I created the magazine Life Cast out of passion. I love special effects in every sense of its meaning. I centered the heart of Life Cast around a few main genres that became the sections in the magazine. Set building, products, wardrobe, and makeup and prosthetics. I dived deep into the rabbit hole to figure out how these things are made and how they are applied to the final product. Life cast is for cosplayers, geeks, movie makers, models, special effects fanatics, makeup artists, and so many more groups of people such as them. It was a long and amazing journey to get to the end of this project. I loved it so much I based a convention on it. The style scape/ brand guide is on pages 18-21. This shows the color palette, target audience, typography, about the company, the company mission, and lifestyle photography. Pages 22-23 are the cover, the table of contents, and contributors page. 24-25 are ads I created for the magazine based on my favorite cosplay artists, Kamui Cosplay, books and my favorite cosplayer, Yaya Han. 26-27 are articles I found and designed for the magazine.

Life Cast Con was born of my need to create a convention for my class and my desire to continue the journey of Life Cast magazine. I created the logo after looking through hours of cryptid photos to see what was common enough to use for a universally understood logo. Thus, the fangs were born. On that same cryptid rabbit hole, I found the convention’s mascot, the Wolpertinger! He is a cute little guy that I frankensteined together myself in photoshop. He is part deer, part owl, part squirrel, and mostly rabbit. Around the time I was finalizing this, I discovered the show, Dragula, and fell in love! The Boulet Brothers encompassed what I wanted for this con so I made them sponsor it for me (sadly not irl) in the print and email flyers for the upcoming event. The next stage was creating the environmental designs, the badge, and the merchandise. I created a selfie wall and flags to help con goers find their way. I love this project with all my heart. Life Cast in general is my love child and definitely my favorite to bring to life from nothing. (Don’t tell my other projects!!) Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Placeit

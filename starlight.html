Starlight Cosmetics

Packaging Design ✦ Merchandise Design ✦ Brand Identity

Valencia College ✦ Sophomore 2019

Starlight is product packaging and merchandise that I made for a made up makeup brand made specifically for this project, Kavan Cosmetics. There are 4 items in the box set, a lip scrub, a highlighter, a blush, and a setting spray. I spray painted all of the makeup black to make the pieces look the same, photoshopped a grey scale photo of the stars with color overlays to get Starlights signature space background, and created all of the boxes and labels through Illustrator then printed, cut out, folded, and glued them all together. The labels for each are my own handwriting that I vectorized. Indesign, Illustrator, Photoshop, Placeit
